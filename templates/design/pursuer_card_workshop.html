How to choose

  • Most distinctive / no-competitor-has-it: B (spectral) > A > C.
  • Most "premium identity artifact you'd screenshot": A > B > C.
  • Hits the trophy-hunter ego hardest: C > A > B.
  • Scales to the small sizes (comment chip, leaderboard row): A > C > B (the spectral band needs width).
  • My lean: A as the base artifact, with B's spectral band woven in as the backdrop — the artifact frame + the ownable face. C's platinum pedestal becomes a feature within it, not the whole card.
