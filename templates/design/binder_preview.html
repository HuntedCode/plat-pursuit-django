Binder Preview

The display piece. Three views: Single (default Frame, vertical scroll) and Compact (chrome-only cards, denser scroll) both stack pages down the page. Spread renders one facing-page spread on screen at a time with a 3D page-flip between spreads — Prev/Next buttons or drag the right/left edge to turn. The Presentation toggle switches the chrome itself: Binder keeps the cover, spine, rings, and pocket sleeves; Gallery strips them for a clean card-grid (mobile defaults here). For finding/sorting specific badges, see the Badge Collection list — same data, hunting-tool shape.
