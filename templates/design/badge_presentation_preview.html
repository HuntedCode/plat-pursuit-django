How should a badge be presented?

The badge art is already a self-contained medallion — a laurel-framed backdrop (tier-coloured), the custom art in the centre, and a tier crest on top. So the real question isn't "does it need a frame" — it already has one. It's: do we box the medallion inside a trading-card Frame, or do we let the medallion be the object? Same real art, four treatments, side by side.

What to look for: Which one makes you want to pick it up? Which respects the artwork vs. competes with it? Which reads as a precious earned object vs. a UI element? And crucially — which scales to a whole collection (scroll to the grids) without feeling like paperwork?
