A · Prestige metals

A rarity climb: slate → bronze → silver → gold → gem → luminous → prismatic. Reads instantly as "collector tiers," leans into the trophy-hunting soul. Most colourful.

B · Spectrum ascent

A smooth hue journey, cool → warm → transcendent. Position reads by hue alone; very legible ordering, more "playful" energy. Risk: further from the restrained brand palette.

C · Tonal cyan ascent

One brand hue (cyan), ascending in brightness/energy from dim to brilliant white. Most on-brand (respects the single-accent rule); subtle — the read is "brighter = higher" rather than distinct colours.
