Why this is the right turn

Both are things a hunter would actually screenshot, because they show the thing they're proud of. The differentiation isn't a metaphor nobody asked for; it's that we present real platinums with reverence (the Frame chrome, the under-glass covers, the Tally numbers, the rank standing) instead of a flat table. My lean: A as the hero/share card (the case is the flex), B as the compact/profile-header. The element rank stays as the standing line; the Lab is where the gamification depth lives for those who want it.
