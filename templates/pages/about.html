About Platinum Pursuit

Our Mission

Platinum Pursuit is a community-driven platform built by trophy hunters, for trophy hunters. We're passionate about celebrating gaming achievements and connecting players who share the same dedication to earning every trophy.

What We Offer

Trophy Tracking

Sync your PSN profile and track your trophy progress across all your games. See your stats, completion percentages, and earning history.

Community Guides

Access trophy guides written by fellow hunters. Share your own strategies and help others conquer challenging trophies.

Leaderboards

Compete with other hunters on our leaderboards. Earn badges and recognition for your achievements.

Trophy Case

Showcase your proudest achievements. Curate a personal trophy case to display your rarest and most meaningful platinums.
