Reviews are taking a break

We've temporarily retired community reviews. During a behind-the-scenes upgrade to how we match games to their IGDB entries, a data-handling bug caused some reviews to be lost. We're not going to sugar-coat it: that data is gone, and we're sorry.

Rather than carry on with a system we no longer fully trust, we're putting reviews away for now and plan to rebuild them properly down the road. If you wrote reviews, thank you. That effort mattered, and losing any of it is exactly the kind of thing we never want to happen.

The same bug touched game ratings too, so a small number of those may have been lost as well. Ratings are simple and easy to redo, though, so we're keeping that system running. If a rating of yours is missing, you can re-add it any time, including through the Rate My Games wizard.

To be clear: reviews aren't gone for good. We fully intend to bring them back, rebuilt to be more reliable than before. Thanks for sticking with us.
