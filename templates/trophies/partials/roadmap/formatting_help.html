{# ─── Callouts ──────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Callouts

Open a blockquote with one of these markers on its first line:

> [!NOTE]
> body Note (info / context) > [!TIP]
> body Tip (strategy) > [!WARNING]
> body Warning (heads-up) > [!IMPORTANT]
> body Important (don't skip)

{# ─── Tips ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Tips

  • Wrap an image in ||...|| for a hidden-by-default image (great for endgame screenshots).
  • Callouts can contain any other markdown — lists, links, controller icons, even spoilers.
  • Tables get horizontal scroll on phone widths automatically. Don't worry about column count.
  • Use #### for "small caps" labels inside long sections.

Keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl+B bold, Ctrl+I italic, Ctrl+U underline.
