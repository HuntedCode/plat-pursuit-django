How badges work

Every badge is a themed set of PlayStation games (a franchise, a studio, a collection). Each has four tiers to collect, and here is how you earn them:

  1. Bronze Platinum PS4 & PS5
  2. Silver 100% PS4 & PS5
  3. Gold Platinum All platforms
  4. Platinum 100% All platforms

Tiers are independent, so grab any without the others. "All platforms" adds VR and classic PS3 and Vita games on top of PS4/PS5.
