Community Guidelines

Please review and follow these guidelines when participating in our community:

Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect. No harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or discriminatory language of any kind.

Keep It Family-Friendly

Avoid NSFW content, explicit language, and mature themes. We want to maintain a welcoming environment for gamers of all ages.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to PlayStation gaming, trophies, and the game being discussed. Avoid off-topic conversations.

No Spam or Self-Promotion

Don't post repetitive content, advertisements, or excessive self-promotion. Share helpful content, not spam.

Use Spoiler Tags

Use spoiler tags ||like this|| when discussing plot points, endings, or major game revelations. Respect others' gaming experiences.

Be Helpful and Constructive

Share tips, strategies, and advice that helps the community. Constructive criticism is welcome, but keep it respectful.

Follow Platform Rules

Adhere to all applicable laws and PlayStation's Terms of Service. Don't share exploits, hacks, or methods to circumvent game systems.

Report, Don't Retaliate

If you see rule-breaking content, report it to moderators. Don't engage in arguments or retaliate with similar behavior.

Protect Privacy

Don't share personal information about yourself or others. Keep PSN usernames public but respect privacy otherwise.
