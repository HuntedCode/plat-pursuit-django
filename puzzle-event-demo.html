Reading this demo: The buttons stand in for community events (a plat, a milestone, a donation) that would unlock pieces in the real version. Tile attribution uses fake usernames sampled from a small pool. Page-load behavior previews what a returning visitor sees: already-revealed pieces appear instantly with no animation. Live reveals fire their selected animation plus a one-shot accent ring around the unlocking piece.
